Happy New Year!

We just drove up to Ramsau near Berchtesgaden to try our hand at tobogganing on the [Hirscheckblitz Rodelbahn](https://www.hochschwarzeck.info/de/hirscheckblitz/hirscheckblitz-landingpage). My hands are freezing and I’m covered in snow, but it was such a fun way to kick of the new year. 

Watching the sun go down over the mountains from the top was worth the trip alone.
